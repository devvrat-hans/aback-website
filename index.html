What is business process automation?

Business process automation is the use of technology to execute recurring tasks or processes in a business where manual effort can be replaced. It streamlines workflows, increases efficiency, reduces costs, and minimizes human error. Our automation solutions at Aback.ai are designed to identify and automate key business processes to help your organization operate more efficiently.
